Game2D

"Game2D" is the first big project im developing. I'm currently working with a 2D artist to get sprites and tilesets drawn up. Using the corona SDK, I am creating an RPG for iPhone and iPad as well as Android phones and tablets. The following images are in early stages of the games development, getting a map, actor and enemy loaded onto the screen. Movement, animations, & dialog boxes are implemented and im actively working on making it better.

Story is starting to be written aswell although the combat is what the highlight of this game will be.

Combat will feature swipe to attack melee and magic attacks. Combining traditional RPG gameplay with touchscreen gestures. The first set of custom tiles are the last two images Some planned features I have include:
  • Swipe to attack with melee slashes or magic attacks if you slide your finger from the character outwards.
  • Level and achievement systems (using the new android game features)
  • Weapon system with different animations and effects for different weapons Different magic attacks & effects
  • Character progression... with weapons, stats, and leveling.


Once released If I get enough support I will take this game to the next level with regular updates.
Tech Used:
  • Corona SDK
  • Million Tile Engine
  • Lua
